Research

Dissertation

Agri-Environmental Governance in the Age of the Plantationocene

My dissertation examines how agricultural governance systems respond to climate change and environmental degradation. I analyze the interplay between mitigation strategies, adaptation policies, and alternative approaches to food production. The work draws on environmental sociology, science and technology studies, and political ecology to understand how governance arrangements shape sustainability outcomes.

Expected completion: 2026

Current Projects

Responsible Innovation in Precision Agriculture

Research assistant on NSF-funded project "Testing a Responsible Innovation Approach for Integrating Precision Agriculture Technologies with Future Farm Workers and Work." I conduct qualitative interviews with farmers and farm workers, participate in collaborative data analysis, and contribute to academic papers and policy briefs on how new agricultural technologies affect labor and livelihoods.

Agricultural Data Governance

Contributing to USDA-funded research examining data governance in agriculture. I design stakeholder workshop strategies exploring the future of agricultural data and lead a component on science communication about agricultural data in museum settings, combining practical exhibition design with academic analysis from a "design ethnography" perspective.

Justice and AI in Agriculture

Ongoing research on how artificial intelligence applications in agriculture can be designed and governed to benefit smallholder farmers and address power asymmetries. This work has resulted in contributions to the UN STI Forum, an Oxford Handbook chapter, and peer-reviewed publications on responsible AI governance in food systems.

Research Themes

Sustainability Transitions

How do societies shift toward more sustainable practices? I study the governance arrangements, institutional dynamics, and policy instruments that enable or constrain sustainability outcomes in agriculture and food systems.

Technology Governance & Policy

My research examines how emerging technologies like AI are governed, particularly in agriculture. I focus on questions of justice, responsible innovation, and the power dynamics embedded in technological systems.

Multispecies Research

Drawing on environmental sociology and animal studies, I explore how human-animal relations are configured in food systems and how these relationships inform broader questions about sustainability and justice.
