Research Interests

Sustainability Transitions & Environmental Governance

How do societies organize the shift toward more sustainable practices? I examine the governance arrangements, policy instruments, and institutional dynamics that shape sustainability outcomes in agriculture and food systems.

AI & Technology Governance

My current research focuses on artificial intelligence in agriculture, examining questions of justice, data governance, and responsible innovation. I study how emerging technologies reshape power relations in food systems.

Science & Technology Policy

Drawing on my consulting experience, I analyze innovation systems, technology transfer, and the relationship between research institutions and industry development, particularly in Latin America.
