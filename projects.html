Interactive & Creative

Foundation Protocol

A narrative game exploring themes of climate futures, institutional change, and collective decision-making. Built using AI coding tools (Claude Code and Google's Jules) as an experiment in what non-programmers can create with emerging development technologies.

The project emerged from my research on sustainability transitions and governance. I wanted to explore how interactive narrative could make abstract policy questions more tangible and engaging.

Policy & Consulting

Mining Suppliers Ranking

14 consecutive years leading Chile's most comprehensive benchmarking tool for mining suppliers. This annual ranking has become an industry standard for evaluating innovation and technology capabilities in the mining supply chain.

Chile's National Technology Roadmap for Food

Led the update of Chile's national technology roadmap for the food sector with Transforma Alimentos. This strategic planning exercise brought together industry, academia, and government to chart technology priorities for Chilean food production.

Negocios Reverdes (Green Businesses)

CORFO-funded regional initiative in Coquimbo promoting circular economy and sustainable business practices. Designed and implemented capacity-building programs for local enterprises transitioning to green business models.
